Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

We take your privacy as seriously as we take our pour-overs. The Octave Coffee app is designed to function completely offline and does not collect or store any personal data.

What We Collect

Absolutely nothing. No names, no emails, no device IDs. Octave is too lazy to track you.

Data Storage

All calculations (like coffee-to-water ratios) happen on your device. We don’t store anything in the cloud or transmit any information anywhere.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics, or advertising tools.

Children’s Privacy

The Octave app is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

That’s it. No fine print. Just coffee.
